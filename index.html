About

We focus on opportunities where capital, structure and execution work together.

HART Europe Holding operates as a private holding and investment company. The company reviews opportunities where financial analysis, legal structure, operational control and experienced local partners can improve risk-adjusted outcomes.

The company is interested in NPL portfolios, distressed and credit-related assets, servicing businesses and operationally intensive situations with documented cash flows, enforceable rights and transparent governance.
